# Power-BI-Projects
## 🏬 Super Store Sales Dashboard

### Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing store performance across customers, orders, regions, and product categories. Built to provide actionable insights into sales trends and regional strengths.

### Key Highlights
- 📦 **Orders:** 4,922  
- 👥 **Customers:** 793  
- 💰 **Regional Sales:** West (0.71M) leads, followed by East (0.67M)  
- 📊 **Category Trends:** Office Supplies dominate orders in the West  
- 📈 **Sales Timeline:** Peak sales in November (Q4)  

### Skills Demonstrated
- Data modeling with Power Query & DAX  
- Regional and category‑based comparative analysis  
- Interactive visuals & storytelling with Power BI  

### Insights
- Strong Q4 seasonality with November peak  
- West region drives highest sales and orders  
- Office Supplies outperform Furniture & Technology in order volume

## 📱 Social Media Analysis Dashboard

### Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard comparing social media platforms by user activity, engagement, growth, and time spent. Designed to highlight platform trends and audience behavior.

### Key Highlights
- 👥 **Monthly Active Users:** Messaging leads with 24% share  
- ⏱️ **Daily Time Spent:** TikTok tops at 58 mins/day  
- 📈 **YoY Growth:** Bluesky +85%, RedNote +45%  
- 🔥 **Engagement Rates:** TikTok ~3%, RedNote ~2.7%, Reddit ~2.5%  

### Skills Demonstrated
- Data modeling with Power Query & DAX  
- Comparative analysis across multiple KPIs  
- Visual storytelling & interactive dashboards  

### Insights
- Short‑form video dominates user attention  
- Emerging platforms (Bluesky, RedNote, Threads) show rapid growth  
- Engagement varies widely, with TikTok far ahead of legacy platforms

## 🛍️ Retail Sales Dashboard

### Overview
An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing retail performance across customers, cities, months, and product categories. Built to highlight KPIs and uncover actionable insights.

### Key Highlights
- 📈 **KPIs:** 33K customers, 50K sales  
- 📊 **Monthly Trends:** December drives 87% of sales  
- 🌆 **City Analysis:** Balanced sales across major U.S. cities  
- 🛒 **Category Insights:** Electronics & Home/Kitchen lead revenue; Books lowest  

### Skills Demonstrated
- Data modeling with Power Query & DAX  
- Interactive visuals & drill‑downs  
- Business storytelling through dashboards  

### Insights
- Seasonal spikes (Nov vs Dec)  
- Pricing gaps between original vs final prices  
- Category & city‑level performance drivers
